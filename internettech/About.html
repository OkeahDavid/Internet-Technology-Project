About the Author

She graduated from the University of Portharcourt and has since pursued a career in writing short stories. She is an upcoming force of nature in the Afrcan scene known for expressing cultural ongoings in the Nigerian society through her literary works. She is known for crafting real stories that will inspire, educate, develop, empower, motivate inform and correct!
